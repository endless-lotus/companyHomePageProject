Our Intention

We are currently seeking to provide the most optimal tasting experience for coffee in the heart of Seattle. Hoping to achieve this through consistency in flavor, temperature, and customer service. To continuously provide the best optimal coffee experience for you!
There is a variable lit cafe welcoming those with different social backgrounds. You will also find a window on the side for fast takeout of your favorite cup of jo. The intention is so this experience talks to all walks of life people. Those on the go and those who need a seldom break to cherish their taste buds. We welcome all and pursue the differences.

How We Started

As three best friends we always loved going out and spending time together. We especially wanted to create a place where people could come together, spend time, and feel comfortable in their ever bustling lives. So we found a place we too could slow down and decided to set up shop!
Ever since 2016 we have been brewing coffee at the heart of Pikes Place. Welcoming guests near and far a place to rest for a solem minutes and enjoy the coffee we all enjoy together. Though this journey sounds easy it did have its trials and luckliy we all had eachother.
